Welcome Letter

We're so excited to have you join us for this unforgettable journey to Taiwan! This trip is all about meaningful exploration, immersive cultural experiences, and most importantly—fun, connection, and adventure for everyone in the group.

We've put together a mix of engaging, interactive activities that will allow everyone to learn about Taiwan and have fun. We want to focus on giving the kids opportunities to explore independently, build confidence, and experience the incredible safety and friendliness that Taiwan is known for.

At the same time, we know how important flexibility is when traveling, so the schedule includes plenty of free time to relax, recharge, or go off and explore on your own. We want you to feel like you're on vacation too!!

With excitement,
Dina and Welly

Daily Itinerary

1

🌇 Day 1: August 2 (Saturday) — Welcome to Taipei

  • Arrival in Taipei + breakfast at the hotel
  • Settle into rooms and explore Dazhi neighborhood
  • Optional: Shrimping adventure
  • 📍 Taipei Marriott Hotel
2

🌿 Day 2: August 3 (Sunday) — Mountains & Markets

  • Scenic drive through Yangmingshan National Park
  • Visit the alpacas!!
  • Beitou Library / Hot Spring Park
  • Group outing to Taipei 101 in the evening
  • Free time to relax or explore
  • 📍 Taipei Marriott Hotel
3

🏮 Day 3: August 4 (Monday) — Magical Jiufen

  • Release sky lanterns and visit waterfalls in Pingxi
  • Explore the lantern-lit charm of Jiufen Old Street
  • Experience the Spirited Away atmosphere
  • 📍 Taipei Marriott Hotel
4

🎭 Day 4: August 5 (Tuesday) — Rest and Urban Wandering

  • Relaxing morning
  • Optional: Huashan 1914 Creative Park
  • Shi-Men Ting: mango shave ice, shopping, boba, claw machines!
  • Night market exploration
  • 📍 Taipei Marriott Hotel
5

😊 Day 5: August 6 (Wednesday) — Free Day/TBD

  • Optional morning U-Bike ride
  • Maybe Tamsui Old Street Fort Santo Domingo
  • Flexible scheduling for personal preferences
  • 📍 Taipei Marriott Hotel
6

🏛 Day 6: August 7 (Thursday) — Palaces & Parks

  • Human Rights Museum
  • Maokong Gondola into the tea mountains (weather permitting)
  • Low-key cultural preparation for overnight trip
  • 📍 Taipei Marriott Hotel
7

🚆 Day 7: August 8 (Friday) — Off to Tainan!

  • Travel south to Taiwan's ancient capital
  • Fort Provintia exploration
  • Confucius Temple visit
  • Anping Tree House & Harbor walk (ice cream!)
  • 📍 Just Sleep Tainan
8

🎶 Day 8: August 9 (Saturday) — Culture & Return

  • Ten Drum Cultural Village
  • Chimei Museum art immersion
  • Travel back to Taipei by evening
  • High speed rail option (advance booking required)
  • 📍 Taipei Marriott Hotel
9

🧵 Day 9: August 10 (Sunday) — Creative Pulse

  • Taipei 101 Observation Deck (weather permitting)
  • Mitsukoshi Shopping Area
  • Songshan Cultural and Creative Park
  • Raohe Night Market with the dog stores
  • 📍 Taipei Marriott Hotel
10

🌾 Day 10: August 11 (Monday) — Yilan Day Trip

  • Head to Yilan for a change of pace
  • Kavalan Distillery tour (adults enjoy, kids welcome)
  • Natural indigo dyeing experience
  • Hot spring relaxation
  • 📍 Taipei Marriott Hotel
11

🏖 Day 11: August 12 (Tuesday) — Beach & Heritage

  • Relax at Baishawan Beach
  • Enjoy the north coast swimming destination
  • Beach activities and relaxation
  • 📍 Taipei Marriott Hotel
12

😊 Day 12: August 13 (Wednesday) — Free Day/TBD

  • Optional morning U-Bike ride
  • Maybe Tamsui Old Street Fort Santo Domingo
  • Personal exploration time
  • 📍 Taipei Marriott Hotel
13

🍜 Day 13: August 14 (Thursday) — Cook Like a Local

  • Hands-on cooking class at CookInn Taiwan
  • Dihua Street exploration
  • Shrimping experience
  • Lungshan Temple visit
  • Snake Alley Huaxi Night Market
  • 📍 Taipei Marriott Hotel
14

✈️ Day 14: August 15 (Friday) — Goodbye for Now

  • Final time in Taipei for packing
  • Last-minute treats and souvenir shopping
  • Relaxing departure preparation
  • 📍 Taipei Marriott Hotel
